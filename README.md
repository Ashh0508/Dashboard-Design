# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze revenue, profitability, customer behavior, geographic performance, and payment preferences.

The dashboard is designed for business stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, evaluate customer value, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data, but raw data alone does not provide actionable insights.

This dashboard was created to answer key business questions:

* How is overall sales performance changing over time?
* Which categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which states contribute the most sales and profit?
* Who are the highest-value customers?
* Which payment methods are most frequently used?
* How profitable is the business overall?

## 📊 Key Insights

* The business generated approximately **$3M in sales revenue**.
* Overall profit reached **$756K**, resulting in a healthy **26.25% profit margin**.
* **Office Supplies** emerged as the highest revenue-generating category.
* **New York** contributed the highest sales and profit among all states.
* **Debit Card** was the most preferred payment method.
* Most customers placed only one order, indicating an opportunity to improve customer retention and repeat purchases.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### Customer Retention

Implement loyalty programs and targeted campaigns to encourage repeat purchases.

### Category Expansion

Increase focus on high-performing categories that drive revenue growth.

### Geographic Strategy

Prioritize marketing and sales efforts in top-performing states.

### Payment Optimization

Maintain and optimize the most frequently used payment methods to improve customer experience.
